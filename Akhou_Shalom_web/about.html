About Me

Designer, student, and creative thinker.

I design with clarity, intention, and a focus on how people actually experience things.

My Story

Hi, I’m Shalom Akhou, a UI/UX designer and web design student with a strong interest in creating digital experiences that feel clear, engaging, and visually intentional.

What first drew me to this field was the balance between creativity and problem-solving. I’ve always been interested in how design shapes the way people feel, interact, and connect with content. Through my studies and personal projects, I’ve developed skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress, wireframing, layout design, and visual branding.

I enjoy building websites that are not only functional, but also reflect a strong visual identity. My approach focuses on clean structure, thoughtful design choices, and creating experiences that are easy for users to navigate.

Outside of web design, I have a strong interest in visual storytelling, fashion, and digital content creation, which has helped shape my eye for aesthetics, audience connection, and personal branding.

What I Value

I value clarity, consistency, and design that feels intentional. I’m drawn to work that solves problems while still feeling visually strong and memorable. I believe strong design should feel effortless to the user, even when the process behind it is complex.

Where I’m Going

I’m continuing to grow my skills in design and front-end development while building a portfolio that reflects both my technical abilities and creative perspective. I’m focused on pushing my work beyond basic execution into something more polished, thoughtful, and distinctive.
